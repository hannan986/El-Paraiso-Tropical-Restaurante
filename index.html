Our Story

A Taste of Paradise, Born from Family Love

El Paraiso Tropical was born in 2018 when Chef Maria Rodriguez and her family brought the vibrant, soulful flavors of the Caribbean to Miami's heart. Raised in Havana, Chef Maria learned to cook at her grandmother's side — mastering the art of slow-braised ropa vieja, perfectly caramelized maduros, and the silkiest flan you have ever tasted.

Every dish on our menu tells a story of heritage, community, and the belief that the best meals are shared with the people you love. We source locally whenever possible, partnering with South Florida farmers to bring you the freshest tropical produce on every plate.
